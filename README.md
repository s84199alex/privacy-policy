# privacy-policy
Privacy Policy for HelperCook
Effective Date: June 13, 2026
This Privacy Policy describes how HelperCook ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").
1. Information Collection
We prioritize user privacy and minimize data collection.
•
Family ID and Password: We store a unique Family ID and passwords to synchronize recipes across devices within your household. These are stored securely in Google Firebase.
•
User Content: Recipes, ingredients, and photos you upload are stored in our cloud database to allow access by your family members and, if you choose, other users via the Recipe Market.
•
Coins/Credits: We track your virtual coin balance (CookCoins) to facilitate in-app features and rewards.
2. Third-Party Services
Our App uses several industry-standard third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:
•
Google Play Services: For App functionality and updates.
•
AdMob: We show advertisements to provide free services. AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID to show relevant ads.
•
Google Firebase: Used for data storage, synchronization, and anonymous authentication.
•
Google ML Kit: Used for on-device language translation. Translation processing happens locally on your device.
3. Usage Data and Security
We do not collect personal identifiers like your real name, phone number, or email address. We use standard industry security measures to protect the Family ID and recipes you store on our servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
4. Children’s Privacy
Our App is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
